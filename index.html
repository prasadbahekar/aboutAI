Evolution of AI

1956 - 1957

The Birth of AI

The Dartmouth Conference marks the inception of AI as an academic discipline.

1980s

Knowledge-Based Systems

Expert systems emerge, using knowledge representation and reasoning to solve complex problems.


1997 - 1998

Deep Blue vs Kasparov

IBM's Deep Blue defeats chess grandmaster Garry Kasparov, showcasing AI's potential in strategic decision-making.

2012 - 2013

ImageNet Breakthrough

AlexNet wins the ImageNet competition, revolutionizing computer vision through deep learning.

2016 - 2017

AlphaGo's Triumph

Google's AlphaGo defeats world champion Lee Sedol, demonstrating AI's ability to master complex games using deep reinforcement learning.

2020s

AI in the Mainstream

AI applications permeate everyday life, from personalized recommendations to autonomous vehicles, marking a new era of practical AI integration.
